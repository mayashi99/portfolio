Who Am I?

I’m Mayashi Manodaya, a Code Enthusiast & Full-Stack Developer By day, I’m a 3rd-year Information Technology student at SLIIT, and by night (or any free moment, really), I’m a passionate Full-Stack Developer crafting digital experiences that blend creativity with functionality. My keyboard is my canvas, and JavaScript, Python, and a sprinkle of magic are my brushes.

I thrive in the dynamic world of React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, building everything from sleek front-end interfaces to robust back-end systems. Alongside development, I am passionate about UI/UX design—crafting intuitive, visually engaging, and user-centered digital experiences. Whether it’s a RESTful API, a real-time web app, or a complete UI/UX redesign, I love turning ideas into scalable, efficient, and user-friendly solutions.

Beyond code, I’m a problem-solver at heart—debugging is my superpower, and clean architecture is my obsession. When I’m not in VS Code, you’ll find me in tech communities, hackathons, or geeking out over the latest frameworks.
